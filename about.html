Our Story

At Fashion Pet Salon, we believe every pet deserves to look and feel their best! Our story began when lifelong animal lover Jamie adopted a scruffy rescue dog named Max. After struggling to find a salon that treated Max like family, Jamie decided to create a warm, welcoming place where pets receive top-notch care and pampering. From humble beginnings in a small neighborhood shop, Fashion Pet Salon has grown into a trusted destination where grooming meets love.
